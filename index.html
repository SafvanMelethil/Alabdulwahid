    Who Are We?

    Al Abdul Wahid family

    The Al-Abdulwahid family of the Al-Kathir tribe, affiliated with the Al-Ardiyat Governorate in the Emirate of Makkah Al-Mukarramah in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, played an effective role, led by its sheikhs and members of its tribe, the Al-Kathir tribe, in supporting and standing firmly towards presenting the pledge of allegiance, loyalty and obedience to our leader and founder of the third Saudi state, King Abdulaziz Abdulrahman Al Saud and his righteous sons, may God have mercy on him and prolong the life of our leader, the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Salman bin Abdulaziz, and the Crown Prince, Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz.

    What is our website for?

    We are a family with roots that span generations, striving to preserve our shared heritage and history.
